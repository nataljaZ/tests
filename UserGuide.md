##	User guide

###	Teacher manual

<b><u>Sign in</u></b>
<br>
There are three methods to sign in into application:
<br>1.	<u>Primary login</u>. Click <b>Sign In</b> tab at the top-right corner to open login form. 
Fill in e-mail and password that you indicated in registration form. Click <b>Log in</b> button or press enter.
<br>2.	<u>Via Facebook</u>. Click <b>Sign In</b> tab at the top-right corner. 
Click <b>Facebook login button</b> and sign in via your Facebook account.
<br>3.	<u>Via Google</u>. Click <b>Sign In</b> tab at the top-right corner. 
Click <b>Google sign in button</b> and sign in via your Google account.
<br>
<b><u>Sign up</u></b>
<br>
In case you want to use primary login. Click <b>Sign Up</b> tab at the top-right corner to open registration form. 
Fill in all fields and click <b>Sign up</b> button. Click <b>Close</b> button to return to the main page.
<br>In case you want to use Facebook or Google login you do not need to sign up. 
You will register automatically when you first time log in via your Facebook or Google account.
<br>NB! After you sign up you will have status “passive” and cannot create tests until admin checks your data and changes status to “active”.
<br>
<b><u>Add test</u></b>
<br>
Only teachers with status “active” can add tests. 
In case you have status “passive”, after you sign in you will see warning message and <b>Add new test</b> button will be disable. 
If you have status “active” after sign in <b>Add new test</b> button will be available. 
Click <b>Add new test</b> button to open form for creating new test. 
Fill in necessary fields and click <b>Add</b> button. Click <b>Back</b> button to return to the previous page.
<br>
<b><u>Edit test</u></b>
<br>
Choose test from the list of all your tests that you want to modify. 
Click <b>Edit</b> button next to the title of the test to open form for test editing. 
Make necessary changes and click <b>Edit</b> button. Click <b>Back</b> button to return to the previous page.
<br>
<b><u>View test</u></b>
<br>
To see how test will look like for students click on the title of the test (it is a link) in the list of all your tests. 
This test will open in new page.
<br>
<b><u>View report</u></b>
<br>
You can export the results of test to Microsoft Office Excel. 
Choose test from the list of all your tests which report you want to see. Click <b>Export</b> button next to the title of the test to open download window.
<br>
<b><u>Delete test</u></b>
<br>
To delete test from the list of all your tests click <b>X</b> button next to the title of the test.
<br>

###	Student manual

<b><u>List of tests</u></b>
<br>
On the start page of application is a list of all available tests. 
Choose test that you want to take and click on the title of this test (it is a link). This test will open in new page.
<br>
<b><u>Take a test</u></b>
<br>
After choosing and clicking on the title of test that you want to take this test will open in new page.
<br>In case you must sign in to take a test you will see  <b>Facebook login button</b> and <b>Google sign in button</b>. 
You must log in via one of these methods. After you sign in via your account of Facebook or Google form for your personal data will fill in automatically. 
You will need to fill in your student code. You can change your personal data in other fields. 
After answering the questions click <b>Save</b> button.
<br>In case you do not need to sign in you will see  form for your personal data and list of questions. 
You must fill in all fields of form with your personal data. After answering the questions click <b>Save</b> button.
